Cairo

Cairo, the capital of Egypt, is known for its ancient monuments like the pyramids and the Sphinx. The city is a blend of modernity and tradition, offering rich cultural experiences. Learn more on Cairo's Wikipedia page.

Alexandria

Alexandria is a Mediterranean port city founded by Alexander the Great. It's known for its Greco-Roman landmarks, the Citadel of Qaitbay, and the modern Bibliotheca Alexandrina. Learn more on Alexandria's Wikipedia page.

Back to Top

Luxor

Luxor is famous for its ancient temples and the Valley of the Kings. It is often referred to as the "world’s greatest open-air museum" because of its well-preserved monuments. Learn more on Luxor's Wikipedia page.
